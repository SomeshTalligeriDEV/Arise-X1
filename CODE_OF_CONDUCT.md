# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in ARISE-X1 a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, trolling or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainers.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
